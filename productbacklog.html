Product Backlog

A product backlog is the refined prioritized lists of features needed. This backlog is used and evolves during the whole Scrum process. It is a list of all the things that could be done by the team, set in the order of their priority. The Product Owner makes the prioritizing decisions and represents the interests and influences of the stakeholders to the project. The Backlog includes: the items, new features, improvement goals, research, possible and known defects and it is used to show how much time and effort is estimated and spent on an item. The most used way to show the priority of an item is by numbering them with ‘points’
